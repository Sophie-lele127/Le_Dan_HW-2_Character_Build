Harry Potter - The Boy Who Lived

Harry Potter, a member of Gryffindor House, is known for his bravery and determination. He overcame numerous challenges throughout his life and ultimately defeated Lord Voldemort.

As a symbol of courage and resilience, Harry embodies the values of Gryffindor, inspiring countless wizards and witches to stand up against darkness and fight for justice.

x

Cedric Diggory - The Noble Hufflepuff

Cedric Diggory, a loyal and hardworking Hufflepuff, was known for his sportsmanship and fairness. He represented Hufflepuff House in the Triwizard Tournament and displayed true integrity.

Cedric’s legacy lives on as a symbol of loyalty and kindness, embodying the core values of Hufflepuff House.

x

Luna Lovegood - The Quirky Ravenclaw

Luna Lovegood, a Ravenclaw student, is known for her intelligence, creativity, and unique perspective on life. Despite being considered eccentric, Luna’s loyalty and wisdom are unmatched.

As a representative of Ravenclaw, Luna inspires others to embrace individuality and value intellect in all its forms.

x

Draco Malfoy - The Ambitious Slytherin

Draco Malfoy, a member of Slytherin House, is known for his ambition and pride in his heritage. While often misunderstood, Draco embodies Slytherin traits like resourcefulness and loyalty to family.

Through Draco’s journey, we see the complexity of Slytherin, reminding us that ambition can lead to growth and redemption.
